WHO ARE WE?

FourFox Inc. is an American multinational conglomerate created in a corporate restructuring on October 2, 2015. It is the parent company of Google and several former Google subsidiaries. The two founders of Google assumed executive roles in the new company, with Kunal Indore serving as President and Jayesh Chavan as CEO. The company is based in Mountain View, California. It has over 72,053 employees as of 2017.
